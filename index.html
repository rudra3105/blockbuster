Learn About Us

Worldwide non-profit charity organization

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Blockbuster Charitable Trust was founded with the vision to uplift underprivileged communities by providing access to essential resources and opportunities. From supporting education and healthcare initiatives to funding social welfare programs, we work tirelessly to bring about meaningful change. Our dedicated team and generous donors make it possible to transform lives every day.
Our mission is to provide holistic support to marginalized groups, promoting equality, education, and well-being. We strive to bridge gaps in healthcare, education, and community development by offering services that directly impact lives.
To create a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to the tools and opportunities they need to live with dignity and hope.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

We ensure that vulnerable communities have access to nutritious meals, combating hunger and malnutrition. Together, we work to create healthier futures for families in need.

Pure Water

Clean water is a basic human right. We bring safe, pure drinking water to remote areas, improving health and preventing waterborne diseases.

Health Care

Through medical camps and healthcare initiatives, we provide essential services and treatments to underprivileged communities, ensuring everyone has access to quality healthcare.

Primary Education

We offer education programs that open doors for underprivileged children, giving them the tools they need to build brighter futures and break the cycle of poverty.

Residence Facilities

We provide shelter to those in need, offering secure and supportive housing for families displaced by disaster or struggling with homelessness.

Social Care

Our social care programs focus on emotional and mental well-being, helping individuals and families overcome adversity and regain stability in their lives.
